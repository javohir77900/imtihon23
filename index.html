ABOUT US

Myreseber is a leading city & entertainment guide filled with handpicked recommendations of what to do in your city, explore hidden gems and be the first to get the latest buzz!

We consist of a constantly growing community of local experts showcasing a range of curated content around events, food, shopping, fashion, sightseeing, local communities, travel and so much more! We help you make your weekend plans, inform you about the best events happening around the city, tell you all about the best places to eat in the city and much much more. Here's a little taste of the plethora of features we offer: You will happily receive expert recommendations tailor made to suit your interests. We'll provide you with city-specific stories and video hubs, local information - a right-in-your-smartphone, ready to be scoured through! We'll be updating and pushing through fresh content every few hours, giving you more than enough time to scan through and make plans to explore your city. You can also like, share and bookmark all your favorite articles and blurb, and store them ready for future lookups! We pride ourselves on being one of the fastest-loading apps out there today so that you'll have a seamless browsing experience. With this and more, all fresh and ready for you guys, this is one platform that's become your lifeline to navigate all corners of your city. Explore away!
